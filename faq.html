faq

in development
fastbrowser was first started being made 2 days ago. it's still fairly new, dont expect it to be perfect, im not an all knowing deity that could make this browser perfect within days.
why no sync or cloud features?
fastbrowser keeps everything local on your machine. no accounts, no cloud sync, no data sent anywhere. total control over your data.
can i import bookmarks from other browsers?
no, bookmarks might be added in a future release, or bookmarks might never be added for less bloat.
why are extensions disabled?
extensions add bloat and create privacy risks. fastbrowser ships with built-in tracker blocking and protections instead. less to manage, more secure.
does it work on mac or linux?
linux support was released on version 4.1.2, mac support will be released one day.
what about mobile?
mobile might be introduced, but definitely not soon, for a successful port and costs to get developer accounts on both Apple and Android is high.
how do i report bugs?
open an issue on github. provide details about what happened and your system.
is fastbrowser open source?
no. fastbrowser is proprietary software. you can use it freely, but the code isn't available, never will be.
will the browser be as bloated as this site?
no. shut up.
will this always be free?
yes. fastbrowser will always be free to download and use, there will also never be microtransactions, nor do you have to donate and support.
