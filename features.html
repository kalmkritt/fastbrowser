features

blocks trackers
stops 100+ tracking services including Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, Mixpanel, Hotjar, Amplitude, Segment, and more from loading.
cache clearing
automatically clears browsing cache on exit. no traces left behind.
fingerprinting protection
prevents sites from building a profile of you based on browser behavior and settings.
cookie limits
restricts third-party cookies by default. only essential cookies are stored.
local storage only
all your data stays on your machine.
true tab isolation
each tab runs in its own renderer process. crashes or issues in one tab won't affect others.
background tab suspension
inactive tabs are automatically throttled to free up memory. they resume instantly when you click back.
gpu acceleration
hardware-accelerated rendering, zero-copy, and oop rasterization for smooth, fast page rendering.
smart memory management
aggressive garbage collection and automatic memory pressure handling keeps ram usage low even with many tabs.
persistent sessions
your cookies and logins are saved locally and survive browser restarts.
search integration
searches route to duckduckgo lite. plain text in the url bar gets searched, not logged.
no malware
fastbrowser is code-signed and regularly scanned. safe to use.
no extensions
extensions are disabled by default. less bloat, fewer privacy risks, faster performance.
you control your data
browsing history, passwords, and form data are stored locally and never leave your computer.
keyboard shortcuts
ctrl+t for new tab, ctrl+w to close, ctrl+l for address bar, ctrl+r to reload, alt+← to go back, alt+→ to go forward, ctrl+1–9 to switch tabs.
